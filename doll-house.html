Doll House explores voyeurism through scenes of surreal domesticity at night. Produced across two states and in twelve homes, it is a phantasmagorical illustration of privacy in the twenty-first century and, through this, it attempts to define the fabricated lives that we live.
Associated Exhibition with Head On photo festival 2016
Breathing Colours Gallery, Balmain, NSW, Australia
